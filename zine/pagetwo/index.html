Popular cloud systems include Netflix, Hulu, Amazon, Google etc. These allow users to access this content digitally, without needing a physical hardrive whether its for commercial or personal use. As Cloud occupies a space often invisible to most, we forget that it actually requires a physical space as well, requiring the construction of large data centers.
